Explanations about indoor flowers

Houseplants not only add a beautiful and pleasant look to the interior of houses and apartments, but also help improve air quality and reduce stress. Plants such as sansevieria (sword), petunia, ficus and anthurium are among the popular flowers to keep in apartments. These plants grow well with proper lighting and watering conditions and refresh and enliven the home space. Also, keeping houseplants as a relaxing and beneficial activity can increase the feeling of satisfaction and creativity in humans.
